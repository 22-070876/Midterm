Bukto

It is a popular dish in Vintar, it is one of the kind of fresh water fish that usually found or catch in our town especially in Surong valley.it is popularly known as "bukto" in Ilokano and it is very expensive because it is seasonal and it is difficult to find. It has a glorious taste and is surely delicious!
